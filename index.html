About

I am a scientific editor and technical writer based in Toronto. My background is in bioengineering (M.S., University of Illinois Chicago), and I spent 13 years at Cactus Communications, editing more than 2,400 peer-reviewed manuscripts across materials science, engineering, and the physical and life sciences.

That editing background shapes how I approach technical writing: I am attentive to audience calibration, information hierarchy, and the difference between what subject-matter experts assume readers know and what readers actually need. I am also a published peer-reviewed author.
